Our Car Insurance Coverage Options

  • Liability Coverage: Protects you from financial liability in the event of bodily injury or property damage caused by an accident where you're at fault.
  • Collision Coverage: Covers the cost of repairing or replacing your vehicle if it's damaged in a collision with another vehicle or object.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Provides protection against non-collision incidents, such as theft, vandalism, fire, and natural disasters.
  • Personal Injury Protection (PIP): Covers medical expenses and lost wages for you and your passengers in the event of an accident, regardless of fault.
